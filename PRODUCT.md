# Product

## Register

product

## Users

- Sam (builder/operator) is the daily user; Wikipedia editors and maintenance gnomes are the intended community audience (launch will be announced in Wikipedia editor groups).
- Context of use: triaging articles for stale time-bound claims, reviewing deterministic detector output, selectively spending a metered research budget, and leaving with a sourced edit to apply on Wikipedia by hand.
- The project is also a portfolio piece: craft quality is part of the product's purpose, not an afterthought.

## Product Purpose

WikiAsOfNow finds Wikipedia sentences whose "as of [year]" reality has likely expired — deterministically, with no LLM anywhere in detection — and helps a human editor turn each finding into a ready-to-make, sourced Wikipedia edit via a selective, metered research assistant. Success for a session: the editor leaves with a compliant, citable update they can apply on Wikipedia themselves.

## Brand Personality

Archival precision — scholarly, calm, typographic. A well-kept reference room: serious about sourcing, zero hype. Time and the decay of facts are a quiet visual motif, never a gimmick. Voice is exact, neutral, lightly warm; never salesy.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboard: metric cards, gradient accents, icon+heading+text card grids, hero numbers.
- AI-slop defaults: cream/parchment body backgrounds, gradient text, uppercase eyebrow kickers above every section, decorative glassmorphism.
- Hacker-tool austerity: terminal-green brutalism or unstyled minimalism that reads hobbyist rather than trustworthy.
- Permitted: respectful familiarity with Wikipedia's typographic seriousness — a companion to the encyclopedia, not a skin clone.

## Design Principles

1. **Evidence before assertion.** Every claim the UI makes carries its provenance — revision IDs, timestamps, sources, audit trail — visible without digging.
2. **The human is the editor.** The tool prepares; it never pretends to act on Wikipedia. Calls to action hand off to the editor, they don't automate the edit.
3. **Deterministic honesty.** What was computed deterministically and what the metered AI assistant produced are visually and verbally distinct, always. Never blur that line.
4. **Keyboard-first triage.** The repeat workflows — scan, review, accept/skip, research — are tuned for hands-on-keyboard speed with visible focus and discoverable shortcuts.
5. **Quiet craft.** Polish lives in typography, rhythm, and exactness, not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA: body text contrast ≥4.5:1, full keyboard operability, visible focus indicators, semantic landmarks, and a `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative for every animation. Beyond AA: keyboard ergonomics for power use — shortcuts for triage actions, focus order tuned for repeat workflows, and no keyboard traps in inputs.
