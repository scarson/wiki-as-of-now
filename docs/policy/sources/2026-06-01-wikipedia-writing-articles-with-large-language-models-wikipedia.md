This page in a nutshell: Don't use
|

Text generated by [large language models](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Large_language_models) (LLMs) [a] often violates several of

[Wikipedia's core content policies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Core_content_policies). For this reason,

**the use of LLMs to generate or rewrite article content is prohibited**,

except for

[[b]](https://en.wikipedia.org#cite_note-2)[basic copyedits](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Basic_copyediting)and

[translation of material from other language Wikipedias](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:LLM-assisted_translation)as outlined below.

- Editors are permitted to use LLMs to suggest
[basic copyedits](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Basic_copyediting)to their own writing, and to incorporate some of them after human review, provided the LLM does not introduce content of its own. Caution is required, because LLMs can go beyond what is asked of them and can change the meaning of the text such that it is not[supported by the sources cited](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Verifiability). Examples of basic copyedits include spelling, punctuation, and capitalization. - Editors are permitted to use LLMs to
[translate articles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Translation)from another language's Wikipedia into the[English Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/English_Wikipedia), but must follow the guidance laid out at[Wikipedia:LLM-assisted translation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:LLM-assisted_translation).

The writing style of some editors may appear similar to the output of LLMs. The imposition of sanctions requires evidence beyond basic stylistic or linguistic indications. When evaluating possible LLM use, it is best to consider the full pattern of the editor's recent edits and whether the edits comply with core content policies.

Persistent use of large language models outside of the above exceptions can result in an editor being [blocked from editing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:BLOCK).[[c]](https://en.wikipedia.org#cite_note-3)
